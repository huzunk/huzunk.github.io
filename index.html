Welcome to our Lab

Welcome to the Laboratory of wheat abiotic stress research, in the College of Agriculture, Northwest A&F University.
We belong to the State Key Laboratory of Crop Stress Biology in Arid Regions.

Introduction

1.Identification of important agronomic traits and root development-related genes in wheat and research on their mechanism of action.
2.Research on the discovery and action mechanism of wheat heat-resistant gene.
3.Wheat stress resistance molecular design and breeding.

Admissions

We recruit masters, doctors and post-doctoral fellows in plant molecular science, plant physiology and bioinformatics all year round.
Interested students are welcome to join!
